Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

COMBINIxHUNTER ("we", "our", "the app") is a geocollection game developed by an independent developer. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: Email address and a unique username you choose. Used for authentication and leaderboard display.
  • Location data: Precise GPS coordinates, collected only at the moment you capture a store, to verify you are physically within 100 meters of it. Location is never accessed in the background and never tracked continuously.
  • Photos: Images you take when capturing a store. Photos are uploaded to our servers and may be displayed in the app's live feed to other users.
  • Game data: Scores, badges, captures, streaks, leaderboard rankings, and progress stats.
  • Diagnostic data: Crash logs, device type, OS version, and app version, collected for debugging via Sentry (error-tracking service).
  • Usage data: Anonymized interaction events (taps, screen views) for improving the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate your account and maintain your session.
  • Verify captures through GPS, timestamp, and image-hash anti-cheat.
  • Display your captures in the live feed, gallery, and global leaderboard.
  • Calculate rankings, award badges, and track daily quests.
  • Improve app performance, fix bugs, and protect against abuse.

3. Social Sharing

When you tap "Share" on a capture or daily hunt, the app generates an image card locally on your device. The image is then passed to your device's native share sheet (Instagram, Messages, Camera Roll, etc.). We do not upload the generated share card to our servers. What you choose to post on a third-party platform (Instagram, X, etc.) is governed by that platform's privacy policy, not ours.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Supabase (authentication, database, photo storage) — hosted on AWS Singapore region. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Sentry (crash and error reporting) — hosted in the EU. Sentry Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps SDK (map rendering) — Google Play services. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign In / Google Sign In / Facebook Login (optional auth methods) — governed by each provider's privacy policy.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your profile, captures, photos, and all associated data are permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

6. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

  • Access: View all your data directly in the app (Profile screen).
  • Export: Request a complete data export via email. We will respond within 30 days.
  • Deletion: Delete your account from the app (Profile → Logout → Delete account) or by emailing us. All data is removed within 30 days.
  • Correction: Edit your username and profile info in the app.
  • Opt-out of diagnostics: Email us to opt out of anonymized usage analytics.

7. Children's Privacy

COMBINIxHUNTER is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided data, contact us and we will delete it immediately.

8. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS 1.3. Photos are stored in access-controlled buckets. Passwords are hashed via Supabase Auth (bcrypt). We use row-level security policies to ensure users can only access their own data.

9. International Transfers

Our servers are located in Singapore (AWS). If you access the app from outside Singapore, your data will be transferred to Singapore for processing. By using the app you consent to this transfer.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified in-app and the "Last updated" date above will change. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
