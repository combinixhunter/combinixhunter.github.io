HOW IT WORKS

Open the map. Find a convenience store near you. Walk up to it. Take a photo — the app verifies your GPS live. The store is yours.

Score points. Earn badges. Complete daily quests. Climb the global leaderboard. Share your catches on Instagram. Build a passport of countries you've explored.

No fiction. No digital monsters. Every store is real, mapped at its actual location. You can't capture from the couch — you earn it with your feet.

WHY

Because 85,000 convenience stores exist and nobody has ever tried to catch them all. Because the world is more interesting when you're looking for something in it. Because konbini are the connective tissue of modern urban life — from Tokyo to Bangkok to Austin to Taipei — and deserve a monument.

Inspired by FlashInvaders. Built for collectors, completionists, and people who can't leave a good map unfilled.
